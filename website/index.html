Merry Christmas!

The ysap yearly tradition of creating or enhancing a Christmas-inspired CLI animation on the terminal with Bash!


You can render this animation on your terminal by running:


# current year
curl xmas.ysap.sh | bash

# previous years
curl xmas.ysap.sh/2025 | bash
curl xmas.ysap.sh/2025 | bash -s -- -s .1 -c 'abcd'
curl xmas.ysap.sh/2024 | bash
